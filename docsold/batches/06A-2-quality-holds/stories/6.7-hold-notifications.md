# Story 6.7: Quality Hold Notifications

## Goal
Notify relevant users when quality holds are created.

## Acceptance Criteria
- When hold created, notification sent to:
  - QC Manager role
  - Users in notify_users
- Notification includes: LP number, Product, Reason, Severity, Link

## Technical
- FR: QC-FR-08
- Toggle: quality_settings.enable_hold_notifications
- Prerequisites: Story 6.6
