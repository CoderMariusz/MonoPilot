# Story 6.19: Certificate of Analysis (CoA) Upload

## Goal
Upload and store supplier CoAs for tracking.

## Acceptance Criteria
- During GRN, upload CoA file (PDF, image)
- CoA linked to: GRN, Supplier, Product, Batch number
- LP detail shows linked CoA for download

## Technical
- FR: QC-FR-21
- Table: certificates_of_analysis
- Supabase storage: coa-documents bucket
- API: POST /api/quality/coas
- Prerequisites: Epic 5 (GRN)
